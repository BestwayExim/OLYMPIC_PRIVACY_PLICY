Privacy Policy for Olympic Compat App

Last updated: 31/05/2024

Welcome to Olympic Compat App. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our fitness-based application available within our fitness office.

1. Introduction

Olympic Compat App is a fitness-based application designed for use within our gym. Our app serves two main functions:

  • Gym-Based Users: Access to food items, telemedicine services, and a gym-related e-commerce store.
  • Facility Rental: Booking and contacting for renting our gym facilities during free times.

We do not collect personal data from users of the Olympic Compat App. This Privacy Policy outlines the details of our data practices.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

We want to assure you that we do not collect, store, or process any personal data from users of the Olympic Compat App.

3. Use of Information

Since we do not collect personal data, we do not use any personal information for any purposes, including but not limited to marketing, analysis, or third-party sharing.

4. Third-Party Services

The Olympic Compat App includes links and access to third-party services such as telemedicine providers and an e-commerce store. Please be aware that these third-party services operate independently and have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party services you access through our app.

5. Facility Rental

Users who wish to rent our gym facilities can book and contact us through the app. We do not collect any personal data during this process. Any communication regarding facility rental will be handled outside the app, and users are responsible for providing any necessary contact information directly through email or other means.

6. Data Security

Even though we do not collect personal data through the app, we take the security of our systems seriously. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect the app and any communication channels used for facility rental inquiries.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at:

  • Email: olympic@gmail.com

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date at the top. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

By using the Olympic Compat App, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy and agree to its terms and conditions.
